The Challenge: A Tale of Two Crises

South Africa faces a critical juncture where high youth unemployment collides with a rapidly evolving digital landscape, creating a profound skills gap that demands urgent action. HOL has uptaken the initiative to empower kids with coding skills in hopes of them becoming the future problem solvers in the society

The Solution: A 3-Day Coding Bootcamp

A mindset-driven and legacy leadership initiative to equip youth with the skills, confidence, and connections to thrive in the digital economy.

Our 3-Day Bootcamp Agenda

Day 1

**Morning:** Arrival, registration, and fellowship. Welcome and keynote address. Panel presentations. **Afternoon:** Lunch break. Breakaway Sessions (Session 1). Q&A with judges.

Day 2

**Morning:** In-depth coding workshops and hands-on projects. **Afternoon:** AI basics and practical applications. Mindset and Legacy Leadership integration discussions. Collaborative sessions and project work.

Day 3

**Morning:** Panel Breakaway Sessions (Session 2). **Afternoon:** Tea break. Review, feedback, and final announcements. Announcement of three winners. Session closes.
